    • About

    The CellProfiler project team is based in the Carpenter Lab at the Broad Institute of Harvard and MIT.
    The project was started by Anne E. Carpenter and Thouis (Ray) Jones in the Sabatini Laboratory (Whitehead Institute for Biomedical Research) and Golland laboratory (MIT's CSAIL).

    CellProfiler has been cited in 3,160 scientific publications as of February 2016 [How to cite]
